The Simpsons

"The Simpsons") is an American animated satirical series, created by Matt Groening for Fox. The animated series is a satirical parody of the lifestyle of the US middle class, embodied in the Simpsons family, which consists of Homer, Marge, Bart, Leesa and Maggie.

Homer

Homer Jay Simpson) is one of the main characters of the animated series "The Simpsons". Homer is a sweet and funny, but sometimes rude and impolite father of the family. He has obvious flaws: fat, bald and not very smart. Often he behaves like a jester, absurdly, selfishly and tactlessly, but still remains likable.
